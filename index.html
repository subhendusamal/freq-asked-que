Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

General Questions
1. What is Zenspec?

Zenspec.tech is a smart gadget finder and comparison platform designed to help students and professionals find the perfect laptop or smartphone based
on their budget, needs, and specifications.

2. How does Zenspec work?

Simply enter your preferences, such as budget, brand, or specifications, and our advanced filtering system will show you the best options. You can also
compare gadgets side by side to make an informed decision.

3. Is Zenspec free to use?

Yes! Our platform is completely free for users to browse, compare, and analyze gadgets.

4. Where does Zenspec get its data from?

We collect data from trusted sources, including official brand websites, verified retailers, and expert reviews, ensuring accurate and up-to-date
information.

5. Does Zenspec provide purchase links?

Yes, we provide links to trusted online retailers so you can directly purchase your chosen gadget at the best price.

About Zenny
1. What can Zenny do?

Zenny is our AI-powered chatbot that helps answer your tech-related questions, explain complex specifications in simple terms, and guide you through the
website.

2. How do I use Zenny to simplify specs?

Just highlight any technical term or specification, and Zenny will provide an easy-to-understand explanation.

User Accounts & Support
1. Do I need to create an account to use Zenspec?

Yes, you need to create an account to browse and compare gadgets . However, creating an account lets you save favorite products and wishlist them.

2. How can I contact support if I have an issue?

You can reach out to us through our Contact Us page or email us at infozenspec@gmail.com

3. Can I request a new gadget to be added?

Yes! If you don’t find a specific gadget, you can send us a request, and we’ll try to add it as soon as possible.
